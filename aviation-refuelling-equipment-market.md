Nozzles facilitate the controlled dispensing of fuel, and expansion joints accommodate movement and temperature variations in fuel lines. Fuel trucks are specialized vehicles equipped for transporting and delivering aviation fuel. Other equipment includes filtration systems and monitoring devices, enhancing efficiency and safety in refuelling operations.</p></p>
