<p><strong>The PETG Copolyester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion</li><li>Blow Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PETG copolyester market encompasses three primary processing types: injection molding, extrusion, and blow molding. Injection molding involves heating PETG and injecting it into molds to create detailed shapes, ideal for complex parts. Extrusion involves forcing melted PETG through a die to produce continuous shapes like sheets or films, widely used in packaging and signage. Blow molding involves inflating a PETG tube within a mold to form hollow objects like bottles, favored for its efficiency and versatility in producing lightweight containers.</p></p>
