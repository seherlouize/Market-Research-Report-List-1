<p><p>Differential pressure flow meters are instruments used to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases by calculating the pressure drop across a constriction in a flow path, such as an orifice plate, venturi tube, or nozzle. As fluid flows through these constrictions, a pressure differential is established, which is directly related to the flow rate. These devices are popular due to their reliability, accuracy, and suitability for a variety of industrial applications, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p><p>The Differential Pressure Flow Meters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
