<p><p>The grape seed oil market is categorized into two main extraction methods: mechanically pressed and chemically extracted. Mechanically pressed oil is obtained through the physical process of pressing grape seeds, retaining more nutrients and flavor, making it a preferred choice for health-conscious consumers.
