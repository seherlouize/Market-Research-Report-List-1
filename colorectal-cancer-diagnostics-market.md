<p><p>The colorectal cancer diagnostics market encompasses various test types, including Fecal Occult Blood (FOB) tests, Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) tests, and Carcinoembryonic Antigen (CEA) tests, among others. FOB tests detect hidden blood in stools, indicating possible cancer, while CTC tests identify cancer cells in the bloodstream, aiding in monitoring disease progression. CEA tests measure specific protein levels that may elevate with cancer presence. Collectively, these diagnostics play a crucial role in early detection, monitoring, and management of colorectal cancer.</p></p>
