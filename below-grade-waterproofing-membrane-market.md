<p><p>The below grade waterproofing membrane market comprises various types that cater to different needs and applications. Hard mortar membranes are rigid and provide robust protection against water intrusion, ideal for foundations. Flexible mortars allow for movement and are best for surfaces subject to shifting. Acrylic esters offer excellent adhesion and UV resistance, suitable for diverse climates. One-component polyurethane membranes form seamless, durable barriers, effectively preventing moisture penetration. Each type serves unique structural requirements in waterproofing solutions.</p></p>
