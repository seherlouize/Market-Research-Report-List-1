<p><p>The rear axle commodity market includes three primary types: lift axles, dead axles, and drive axles. Lift axles are designed to raise or lower for load adjustments, enhancing vehicle maneuverability and reducing tire wear. Dead axles, which do not provide power, support weight and stability, often used in trailers. Drive axles transmit engine power to the wheels, crucial for vehicle propulsion. These axle types serve various applications in freight transport and heavy-duty vehicles, impacting performance and efficiency.</p></p>
