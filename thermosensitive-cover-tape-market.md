<p><ul><li>Disc Type</li><li>Reciprocating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermosensitive cover tape market is segmented into two main types: disc type and reciprocating type. Disc type cover tapes employ a circular design to efficiently seal and protect components, offering consistent thermal sensitivity for precise applications. In contrast, reciprocating type cover tapes utilize a back-and-forth motion to provide adaptable sealing solutions, catering to varying production speeds and requirements. Both types are essential in electronics packaging, ensuring optimal performance and protection in temperature-sensitive environments.</p></p>
