<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier Detector</li><li>Flat-Panel Imager Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CBCT systems market features two primary types of image detectors: image intensifier detectors and flat-panel imager detectors. Image intensifier detectors amplify the X-ray signal, providing high-quality images for diagnostic purposes but often requiring larger, bulkier components. In contrast, flat-panel imager detectors utilize advanced digital technology, offering superior image quality in a more compact design, benefiting from faster image acquisition and reduced radiation dose. Both types cater to varying clinical needs in dental, orthopedic, and maxillofacial imaging.</p></p>
