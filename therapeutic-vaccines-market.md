<p><p>The therapeutic vaccines market encompasses three primary types: Peptide Therapeutic Vaccines, which utilize specific peptide sequences to elicit an immune response against cancer or infectious diseases; Cell Therapeutic Vaccines, involving the use of whole cells, often derived from tumors or pathogens, to stimulate the immune system; and Nucleic Acid Therapeutic Vaccines, which employ DNA or RNA to instruct cells to produce antigens that provoke an immune response.
