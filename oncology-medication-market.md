<p><p>The oncology medication market comprises several types of treatments. Chemotherapy medications target rapidly dividing cancer cells, often affecting healthy cells as well. Targeted therapy medications focus on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, enhancing efficacy while reducing side effects. Immunotherapy medications boost the body's immune response against cancer cells. Hormonal therapy medications manipulate hormone levels to slow or stop cancer growth, particularly in hormone-sensitive tumors. Other therapies may include supportive care drugs or emerging treatment modalities, broadening management options in oncology.</p></p>
