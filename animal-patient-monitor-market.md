<p><strong>The Animal Patient Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Non-invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal patient monitor market is divided into invasive and non-invasive types, each serving distinct monitoring needs. Invasive monitors require direct intervention, often involving devices that measure parameters like blood pressure through catheters or sensors placed inside the body, enabling precise and immediate data. In contrast, non-invasive monitors gather information externally, such as heart rate and oxygen saturation, using techniques like electrocardiography and pulse oximetry. Both types play crucial roles in veterinary care, ensuring the health and well-being of animals.</p></p>
