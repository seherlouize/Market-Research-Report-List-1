<p><p>The Graphite Felts market is primarily categorized into Rayon-based, PAN-based, and other types. Rayon-based graphite felts are derived from regenerated cellulose, offering excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. PAN-based felts, made from polyacrylonitrile, provide superior resistance to heat and chemicals, catering to demanding industrial needs. Other types may include blends or alternative materials that serve niche applications. Each type is tailored to specific requirements in industries like aerospace, automotive, and energy.</p></p>
