<p><p>The Short Wavelength Infrared (SWIR) sensors market is primarily categorized into line-scan and area-scan types. Line-scan sensors capture images by scanning across a scene one line at a time, making them ideal for applications like industrial inspections and quality control where continuous monitoring of moving targets is essential. In contrast, area-scan sensors capture a full image at once, offering high-resolution imaging for applications such as surveillance and imaging diagnostics. Both types leverage the unique properties of SWIR wavelengths for enhanced visibility and material differentiation.</p></p>
