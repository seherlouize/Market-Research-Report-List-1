<p><ul><li>Collagen Casing</li><li>Cellulose Casing</li><li>Fibrous Casing</li><li>Plastic Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial casings market encompasses various types used in food processing, particularly meat products. Collagen casings are derived from animal hides, offering versatility and a natural look. Cellulose casings, made from plant fibers, are primarily used for products like hot dogs and require peeling after cooking. Fibrous casings, made from a composite of natural and synthetic fibers, are suitable for larger sausages and provide durability. Plastic casings are moisture-resistant, often used for products needing extended shelf life, like vacuum-packed meats.</p></p>
