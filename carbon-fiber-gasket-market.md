<p><p>The carbon fiber gasket market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Plain washers provide a flat, supportive base for sealing, while corrugated gaskets utilize wavy surfaces to enhance compression and sealing effectiveness. Toothed gaskets feature serrated edges that improve grip and prevent leakage under high pressure, and ring gaskets, designed as circular seals, ensure a tight fit in flanged joints. Each type offers distinct advantages in durability, flexibility, and resistance to extreme conditions, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
