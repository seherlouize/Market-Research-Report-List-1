<p><p>The compact benchtop centrifuge market is primarily categorized into ventilated and refrigerated centrifuges. Ventilated centrifuges are designed for routine applications, providing effective sample separation at ambient temperatures, making them ideal for general laboratory work. Refrigerated centrifuges, on the other hand, maintain low temperatures during centrifugation, preventing sample degradation and ensuring the integrity of heat-sensitive materials. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs, offering flexibility and efficiency in various research, clinical, and industrial settings.</p></p>
