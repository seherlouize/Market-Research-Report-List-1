<p><ul><li>Indirect Tire Pressure Monitoring System</li><li>Direct Tire Pressure Monitoring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) enhance vehicle safety and efficiency by providing real-time tire pressure data. There are two main market types: Indirect TPMS, which estimates tire pressure using ABS sensors and wheel speed, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity; and Direct TPMS, which uses sensors in each tire to measure and transmit precise pressure readings directly to the vehicle's display. Both systems aim to prevent tire-related accidents, improve fuel efficiency, and extend tire life through timely alerts and monitoring.</p></p>
