<p><strong>The Football Cleats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SG</li><li>AG</li><li>FG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Football cleats are designed for specific playing surfaces, categorized into three main types: SG, AG, and FG. SG (soft ground) cleats feature longer, removable studs ideal for wet and muddy conditions, providing enhanced traction. AG (artificial ground) cleats are optimized for synthetic turf, featuring shorter, conical studs that reduce the risk of injury while ensuring stability. FG (firm ground) cleats are versatile, designed for natural grass fields, with a blend of conical and bladed studs for optimal grip on firm surfaces.</p></p>
