<p><p>The laparoscopic instrument system market includes various types designed for minimally invasive surgeries. Hook instruments are utilized for grasping and retracting tissues. Monopolar devices use a single electrode for cutting and coagulating tissues, while bipolar instruments provide controlled energy delivery through two electrodes. Blade instruments are designed for precision cutting, and loop instruments typically facilitate suturing and knot-tying. Other instruments may encompass specialized tools for different surgical applications, enhancing versatility and efficiency in laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
