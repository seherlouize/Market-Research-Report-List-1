<p><p>Corrugated tube heat exchangers come in two main types: single tube and multi tube. The single tube type features a single corrugated tube that facilitates efficient heat transfer through its enhanced surface area, ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, the multi tube type consists of multiple corrugated tubes, allowing for greater heat exchange capacity and improved thermal performance. This design is suitable for larger systems requiring higher heat transfer rates, making it versatile across various industrial applications.</p></p>
