<p><strong>The Liquid Glucose Syrups Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Conversion 20-35 DE</li><li>Intermediate Conversion 35-55 DE</li><li>High Conversion 55-70 DE</li><li>Very High Conversion 70-98 DE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid glucose syrups market is categorized based on dextrose equivalent (DE) levels, which indicate the sweetness and conversion degree of the syrup. Low conversion syrups (20-35 DE) are less sweet, suitable for applications requiring mild sweetness. Intermediate conversion (35-55 DE) offers a balanced sweetness and is often used in food products. High conversion syrups (55-70 DE) provide increased sweetness and viscosity, ideal for confectionery. Very high conversion syrups (70-98 DE) are intensely sweet, commonly used in specialized applications like beverages and desserts.</p></p>
