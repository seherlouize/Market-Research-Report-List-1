<p><p>The online car-hailing service market comprises various types including carpooling and carsharing. Carpooling involves multiple passengers sharing a single ride to reduce costs and emissions, often arranged through apps that connect riders with similar routes. In contrast, carsharing allows individuals to rent vehicles on a short-term basis, providing flexible access to cars without long-term ownership. Both models enhance mobility options, promote sustainable transport, and optimize vehicle utilization, catering to different consumer needs and preferences in urban environments.</p></p>
