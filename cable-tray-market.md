<p><p>The cable tray market encompasses several types designed for specific applications. Ladder type cable trays consist of two side rails connected by rungs, ideal for air circulation and heat dissipation. Tray type cable trays, with a solid bottom, provide support and protection for cables in various environments. Large span cable trays are designed to span longer distances while maintaining structural integrity. Combined type cable trays integrate features of both ladder and tray types, offering versatility for complex installations.</p></p>
