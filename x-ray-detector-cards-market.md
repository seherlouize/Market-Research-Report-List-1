<p><p>X-ray detector cards are crucial in medical imaging and industrial applications, categorized as dual-energy, single-energy, and multi-energy types. Single-energy detectors capture images using one energy level, focusing on specific densities. In contrast, dual-energy detectors utilize two energy levels, enhancing contrast and tissue differentiation. Multi-energy detectors further expand this capability by assimilating data from various energy levels, allowing for advanced material characterization and improved diagnostic imaging. This segmentation meets diverse imaging needs, driving innovation and efficiency in the market.</p></p>
