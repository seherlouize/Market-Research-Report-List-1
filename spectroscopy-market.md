Molecular spectroscopy focuses on analyzing the interaction of light with molecules to determine their structure and composition, widely used in chemistry and biochemistry. Atomic spectroscopy examines the light emitted or absorbed by atoms, essential for elemental analysis in materials science and quality control. Together, these techniques drive advancements across multiple scientific disciplines.</p></p>
