<p><p>PCIe switch chips facilitate multiple devices to connect to a host through a single PCIe interface, enhancing bandwidth and flexibility. The market includes various PCIe generations: PCIe 2.0 provides up to 5 GT/s, while PCIe 3.0 offers 8 GT/s with better encoding efficiency. PCIe 4.0 doubles this to 16 GT/s, enhancing data transfer speeds for SSDs. PCIe 5.0 further increases throughput to 32 GT/s, and PCIe 6.0, leveraging PAM4 signaling, aims for 64 GT/s, supporting advanced applications and storage solutions.</p></p>
