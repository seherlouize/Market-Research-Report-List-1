<p><p>The commercial and household water purification systems market includes various types of filtration technologies. Activated carbon filter media effectively remove contaminants and improve taste. Ion exchange resin media softens water by replacing hardness ions with sodium. KDF filter media utilizes redox reactions to reduce heavy metals and bacteria. Other media filtration includes various materials for specific contaminant removal. Reverse osmosis membranes provide thorough filtration by separating impurities based on size. UV disinfection uses ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens, ensuring safe drinking water.</p></p>
