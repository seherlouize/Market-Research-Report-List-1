<p><p>The ice cream filling machine market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and linear machines. Rotary ice cream filling machines feature a circular design that allows for continuous operation, usually increasing efficiency and output. They are ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, linear ice cream filling machines operate in a straight line, offering flexibility and simplicity in setup, making them suitable for smaller batches or varying product sizes. Both types play crucial roles in meeting diverse production needs in the ice cream industry.</p></p>
