<p><strong>The Chloroprene Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acetylene Method</li><li>Butadiene Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chloroprene rubber market primarily comprises two production methods: the acetylene method and the butadiene method. The acetylene method involves polymerizing acetylene with chlorine, resulting in high purity and superior properties suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, the butadiene method utilizes butadiene as a feedstock, providing a cost-effective alternative but with variations in quality and performance. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including adhesives, coatings, and automotive components, influencing the market dynamics and pricing strategies.</p></p>
