<p><strong>The Mechanical Music Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cylinder Movement Type</li><li>Disc Movement Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical music box market is primarily divided into two types: cylinder movement and disc movement. Cylinder movement types utilize a rotating cylinder with pinned protrusions that strike metal tines to produce melodies. They are traditionally crafted and often showcase intricate designs. In contrast, disc movement types employ a flat disc with raised bumps that interact with the tines. This system allows for easier mass production and interchangeable discs, appealing to a broader audience seeking variety and novelty in musical selections.</p></p>
