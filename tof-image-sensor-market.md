<p><strong>The ToF Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) image sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Direct ToF sensors and Indirect ToF sensors. Direct ToF sensors measure the direct time taken for light to travel to the object and back, enabling highly accurate depth mapping in real-time applications such as smartphones and autonomous vehicles. In contrast, Indirect ToF sensors utilize light modulations to determine depth through phase shifts, often being used in applications needing lower cost and complexity, like consumer electronics and gaming.</p></p>
