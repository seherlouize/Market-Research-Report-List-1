<p><p>The Smart Solar Energy Storage System market features two primary types: AC Coupled Solar Batteries and DC Coupled Solar Batteries. AC Coupled systems connect batteries to the grid or inverter via alternating current, allowing for retrofitting existing systems and greater flexibility in energy management. In contrast, DC Coupled systems connect batteries directly to the solar panels, optimizing energy efficiency and performance by minimizing conversion losses. Both types enhance energy storage capabilities, promoting the use of renewable energy and grid independence.</p></p>
